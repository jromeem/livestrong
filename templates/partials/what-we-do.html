Since 1997, we’ve listened to cancer patients, survivors, caregivers, health care providers and researchers on how we, as an organization and a society, can better serve the cancer community.

See our research

Our priorities

Helping cancer survivors and their loved ones has been our goal from day one. We believe in putting the patient first, and that is why we created tools and resources to help ease the challenges of a cancer diagnosis.

 

Our reach

Our innovative partnerships have helped us expand our reach to cancer survivors nationally and beyond. We constantly push to find new and innovative solutions to the day-to-day concerns and challenges they face.

 

Our impact

We are transforming the way the world fights cancer by partnering with best-in-class cancer institutions, politicians and volunteers, and by launching the LIVESTRONG Cancer Institutes, a new, bold endeavor with the Dell Medical School at The University of Texas at Austin.
